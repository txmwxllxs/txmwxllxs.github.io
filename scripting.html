Ongoing

So, I play a video game called Old School Runescape which is commonly reffered to as OSRS. This is a game that I have been playing since the age of 5-6 Years old when my family got their first computer. As the years went on and I got more knowledge of what you can do with code, I thought to myself why dont I try and automate this.

As it turns out, I wasnt the only one who thought this. There was a whole community devoted to creating these bots and I wanted in. Fast forward a couple years to 2019 and I thought that I should finally dip my toes into the dark underbelly of the game community and create these scripts that would allow people to run said scripts for hours on end and make in-game cash to then sell for real life cash.

Being in this community proved to be challenging as the Game's company Jagex actively fought against bots for obvious reasons as they are detrimental to their game as these "bots" bring in a lot of currency into the game thats unjust. This only made scripting more exciting as it felt like running from the police, it also forces you to be serious about the art to write the most flawless, human like scripts you can.

If you would like to know more you can check out my youtube channel where I have uploaded a video detailing one of my first scripts.
